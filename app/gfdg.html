abm Inc. was founded 20 years ago as the first in the industry to introduce cell immortalization technology, products, and services. This achievement established abm as a pioneer and dominant leader in the cell immortalization field, culminating in a Science Journal technique report published on Nov. 21, 2014.

Over the past two decades, we have successfully developed the most comprehensive library of over 600 unique cell lines derived from human, mouse, and major pet primary cells. These cell lines have supported cutting-edge research and drug development in both the healthcare and pet industries. Examples include:

  • Skin cells for the beauty industry
  • Mast cells for allergy research
  • Dermal papilla cell lines from androgenetic alopecia for hair growth research and drug development
  • Cow and chicken muscle cells for in vitro meat production
  • Immortalized human hepatocytes for toxicity studies in new drug development

In addition to our extensive catalog, we also offer custom cell immortalization services to meet your specific needs. If you have any primary cells requiring immortalization, our team is ready to assist ( Cell Immortalization Service ).
